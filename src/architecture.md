Frontend Architecture (Feature-Sliced)

This document describes the high-level folder structure generated for the production-ready
MERN social app frontend. The structure follows Feature-Based and Feature-Sliced Design
principles, focusing on separation of concerns, modular boundaries, and scalability.

See the repository src/ folder for the full tree.

Naming conventions and responsibilities are intentionally minimal here — see the final
deliverable from the assistant for quick reference.
